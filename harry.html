About Harry Potter

Harry Potter is a fictional character and the hero of the wizarding world of Harry Potter created by J.K. Rowling. He is an English half-blood wizard. He is the only son to James Potter and Lily Potter (nee Evans) and Godson of Sirius Black. He was born on 31st July 1980.
Harry Potter attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardy. He was an accomplished Quidditch star and perfect at Defence against the Darks Arts, being able to create a corporeal patronus at the minor age of 13 years. He grew up to end Voldemort in 1998. He took up a position as Head of Auror Office and Head of Magical Law Enforcement.
He was and still remains man crush of a large audience. His wife is Ginevra/Ginny Potter (nee Weasley).
